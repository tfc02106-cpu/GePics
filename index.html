💡 Why did the text become blurry?
If the selected card area is small in the source image, rectification enlarges it. Enlarging low-resolution text makes blur more visible. This version preserves the card aspect ratio automatically and adds an optional sharpening step.

Recommended shooting tips: move the camera closer, focus on the text, use uniform lighting, and make the card occupy more pixels in the source image.
